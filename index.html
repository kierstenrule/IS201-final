About Kiersten

Bio

Kiersten Rule is a senior in BYU's Finance Program. She is from Mesa, Arizona and enjoys singing, running, and baking. She is excited to begin attending law school after she graduates in April. While at BYU, Kiersten has worked as a TA for Physical Science 100, Finance 402, and Finance 201. She is looking forward to the opportunity to continue as a lead TA for Finance 201 next semester.

This past summer, Kiersten had the opportunity to complete an internship at Snell and Wilmer, a business law firm headquartered in Phoenix, Arizona. While there, she was able to gain valuable skills and experience. This coming summer, she is hoping to complete an internship in Washington D.C. where she will be able to learn even more, and she'll be able to do it in the nation's capital.
